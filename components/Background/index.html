Background

Easily apply background colors, gradients, and images to your elements.

Usage

<div class="uk-background-primary uk-light uk-padding">
  <p>Primary Background</p>
</div>

Examples:

Default Background

<div class="uk-background-default uk-dark uk-padding">
  <p>Default Background</p>
</div>

Muted Background

<div class="uk-background-muted uk-dark uk-padding">
  <p>Muted Background</p>
</div>

Primary Background

<div class="uk-background-primary uk-light uk-padding">
  <p>Primary Background</p>
</div>

Secondary Background

<div class="uk-background-secondary uk-dark uk-padding">
  <p>Secondary Background</p>
</div>

Component Options

Class Description
uk-background-default Applies the default background color.
uk-background-muted Applies a muted background color.
uk-background-primary Applies the primary background color.
uk-background-secondary Applies the secondary background color.

Accessibility

Ensure that the contrast between your background and foreground content is sufficient for readability. When using background images, consider adding a background color as a fallback for users who may have images disabled. The uk-light and uk-dark classes from the Inverse component can be used to adjust the color of text and other elements to ensure they are visible on different backgrounds.
