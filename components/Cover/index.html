Cover

A component for creating a responsive, full-height cover image or video.

Usage

<div class="uk-cover-container">
    <img src="https://getuikit.com/docs/images/dark.jpg" alt="" data-uk-cover>
</div>

Example:

iframe

A cover can also contain an iframe.

<div class="uk-cover-container" style="height: 300px;">
