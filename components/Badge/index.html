Component Options

There are no component options for badges.

Accessibility

Badges are purely visual and provide no additional functionality. Ensure that the information conveyed by the badge is also available to users of assistive technologies. For example, if a badge is used to indicate a new item, consider adding visually hidden text that says "New" to the item as well.
