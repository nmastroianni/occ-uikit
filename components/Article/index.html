Article

Create articles within your page, including a title, meta text, and lead paragraph.

Usage

<article class="uk-article">
  <h1 class="uk-article-title">Article Title</h1>
  <p class="uk-article-meta">Written by John Doe on 12 April 2024.</p>
  <p class="uk-text-lead">This is the lead paragraph of the article.</p>
  <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit...</p>
</article>

Example:

Article Title

Written by John Doe on 12 April 2024.

This is the lead paragraph of the article. It is slightly larger than the rest of the text.

Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate velit esse cillum dolore eu fugiat nulla pariatur. Excepteur sint occaecat cupidatat non proident, sunt in culpa qui officia deserunt mollit anim id est laborum.

Component Options

Class Description
uk-article Defines the article container.
uk-article-title Styles the article title.
uk-article-meta Styles the meta text of the article.
uk-text-lead Creates a lead paragraph with a larger font size. From the Text component.

Accessibility

Using proper semantic HTML, like the <article> and <h1> tags, is crucial for accessibility. This helps screen readers and other assistive technologies understand the structure of your page and the hierarchy of the content.
