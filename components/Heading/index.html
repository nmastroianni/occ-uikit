Accessibility

The heading classes are for styling purposes only. To ensure your content is accessible and properly structured for screen readers and search engines, you should always use the appropriate semantic HTML heading elements (e.g., <h1>, <h2>, <h3>, etc.) to represent the hierarchy of your content.
