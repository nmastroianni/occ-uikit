Container

A component for creating a responsive, centered container.

Usage

<div class="uk-container">
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</p>
</div>

Example:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.

Component Options

The container component has a number of options available.

Sizes

A container can have different sizes.

<div class="uk-container uk-container-xsmall uk-border uk-text-center">
    <p>X-Small (750px)</p>
</div>
<div class="uk-container uk-container-small uk-border uk-text-center">
    <p>Small (900px)</p>
</div>
<div class="uk-container uk-container-large uk-border uk-text-center">
    <p>Large (1400px)</p>
</div>
<div class="uk-container uk-container-xlarge uk-border uk-text-center">
    <p>X-Large (1600px)</p>
</div>
<div class="uk-container uk-container-expand uk-border uk-text-center">
    <p>Expand</p>
</div>
